Our Story

Founded in 1995, Greenwood International School began as a small educational institution with just 120 students and 15 faculty members. What started as a modest endeavor has grown into one of the region's most prestigious educational institutions, recognized for its academic excellence and holistic approach to education.

Our campus, spanning over 15 acres of beautifully landscaped grounds, provides an ideal environment for learning, creativity, and personal growth. From our humble beginnings, we have consistently expanded our facilities and programs to meet the evolving needs of our students and the demands of a rapidly changing world.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide a transformative educational experience that nurtures intellectual curiosity, fosters creativity, and develops responsible global citizens. We are committed to academic excellence while cultivating emotional intelligence, ethical values, and social responsibility in every student.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a premier educational institution that empowers students to achieve their fullest potential and make meaningful contributions to society. We envision a learning community that inspires innovation, embraces diversity, and prepares leaders for the challenges of tomorrow.
