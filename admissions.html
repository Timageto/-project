Admissions Process

We're delighted you're considering Greenwood International School for your child's education. Our admissions process is designed to be transparent, straightforward, and supportive as we get to know your family and your child's unique potential.

Our 5-Step Admission Process

1

Application Submission

Complete our online application form and submit required documents. Our admissions team will review your submission within 5 business days.

2

Campus Tour

Schedule a personalized campus tour to experience our facilities and meet our faculty. Virtual tours also available.

3

Student Assessment

Age-appropriate assessments help us understand your child's academic level and learning needs.

4

Family Interview

A conversation with our admissions team to ensure Greenwood is the right fit for your family's values and goals.

5

Admission Decision

Receive notification of admission status within 10 business days of completing all steps.

Admission Requirements

To ensure a smooth application process, please review and prepare the following documents:

For All Applicants

  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate (original + copy)
  • 2 passport-sized photographs
  • Previous 2 years academic records
  • Medical immunization records

Transfer Students

  • Official transcripts from current school
  • Transfer certificate (original)
  • Disciplinary records (if applicable)
  • Special needs documentation (if applicable)
  • Teacher recommendations (2 required)

International Students

  • Passport copy
  • Student visa documentation
  • English proficiency test results
  • Notarized translation of academic records
  • Guardian information (if applicable)

Scholarships & Financial Aid

Greenwood International School offers several scholarship opportunities to recognize and support outstanding students:

Academic Excellence Scholarship

Eligibility: Top 5% of applicants based on entrance assessment

Award: Up to 50% tuition waiver for first year, renewable based on performance

Deadline: March 31 for following academic year

Arts & Talent Scholarships

Eligibility: Demonstrated excellence in visual arts, music, dance, or theater

Award: 25-40% tuition waiver

Requirements: Portfolio review or audition

Sports Scholarships

Eligibility: Competitive athletes in school-sponsored sports

Award: 20-35% tuition waiver

Requirements: Tryouts and coach recommendations

Need-Based Financial Aid

Eligibility: Families demonstrating financial need

Award: Variable based on need assessment

Requirements: Complete financial disclosure forms
