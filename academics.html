Our Academic Philosophy

At Greenwood International School, we believe in fostering intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, and a lifelong love of learning. Our academic programs are designed to challenge students while providing the support they need to succeed. We combine rigorous academics with innovative teaching methods to prepare students for the challenges of higher education and beyond.

Academic Programs

Primary School (Grades 1-5)

Our primary program builds strong foundations in literacy, numeracy, and scientific thinking through hands-on, inquiry-based learning. We focus on developing the whole child with a balanced approach to academics, social-emotional learning, and creative expression.

Learn More

Middle School (Grades 6-8)

The middle years program emphasizes critical thinking, collaboration, and the development of independent learning skills across all subject areas. Our interdisciplinary approach helps students make meaningful connections between subjects and the real world.

Learn More

High School (Grades 9-12)

Our rigorous high school curriculum prepares students for university and beyond, with Advanced Placement courses and specialized tracks in STEM, Humanities, and Business. Students benefit from personalized college counseling and career guidance.

Comprehensive Curriculum

Our curriculum is designed to meet international standards while incorporating local relevance. We offer a wide range of subjects to cater to diverse interests and learning styles.

Mathematics

From basic arithmetic to advanced calculus, our math program develops strong problem-solving skills and logical thinking.

Sciences

Hands-on experiments and inquiry-based learning in physics, chemistry, biology, and environmental science.

Languages

English, Hindi, French, and Spanish with emphasis on literature, composition, and communication skills.

Social Studies

History, geography, civics, and economics to develop global awareness and civic responsibility.

Technology

Computer science, coding, robotics, and digital literacy for the 21st century.

Arts

Visual arts, music, theater, and dance to nurture creativity and self-expression.

Physical Education

Sports, fitness, and health education for physical well-being and teamwork.

Life Skills

Critical thinking, communication, and emotional intelligence for personal development.

Academic Departments
